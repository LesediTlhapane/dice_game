ðŸŽ¯ Game Rules

Objective

Correctly predict whether the sum of two dice will be:

HIGH: 8, 9, 10, 11, or 12
LOW: 2, 3, 4, 5, or 6
SEVENS: Exactly 7
Betting & Payouts

HIGH/LOW Bets: 1-to-1 payout (win double your bet)
SEVENS Bet: 4-to-1 payout (win 4 times your bet)
Bank: Covers all bets - if bank runs out, players win!
Special Rules

Seven Rule: When a 7 is rolled, the dice pass to the next player
Player Bust: If a player runs out of money, they're out
Bank Bust: If the bank runs out, the current player wins
